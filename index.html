Chicken

Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken is a timeless classic that embodies simplicity and sophistication. This dish features a whole chicken marinated and roasted with a blend of fresh herbs, lemon, garlic, and butter, resulting in a tender, juicy, and flavorful centerpiece. The roasting process enhances the chicken’s natural flavors, while the added ingredients create a harmonious balance of savory, tangy, and aromatic notes.
